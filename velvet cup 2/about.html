At Velvet Cup Café, we’re a close-knit team of coffee enthusiasts and food lovers who believe in creating more than just a place to grab a drink—we’re here to offer a little escape from the everyday. For us, it’s all about the experience: from the first warm sip of a perfectly brewed coffee to the cozy, inviting atmosphere where you can relax, recharge, and connect with others. We love what we do, and it shows in every cup we serve, every pastry we bake, and every detail we pour into making our café a place you’ll want to return to. Whether you're here for a quick pick-me-up or to savor a leisurely afternoon, we're excited to welcome you and share the joy of simple, delicious moments.

Our Vision

Our Vision is simple: to create a warm, welcoming space where everyone can enjoy great coffee, delicious food, and feel right at home. We’re here to bring a little joy to your day, whether you’re grabbing a quick cup or relaxing with friends. It’s all about comfort, quality, and making every visit feel special.
